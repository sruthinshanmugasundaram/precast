OVERVIEW

Precast Liquid Storage Tanks are classified into two categories:
Water Tanks and Special Purpose Tanks (Other Liquid Storage Tanks).

WATER TANKS

SPECIAL PURPOSE TANKS

WATER TANKS

Water Tanks are generally used to store water for various purposes. Common types of water tanks based on their usage are:

Potable Water Tanks

Used in residential, commercial, and industrial premises to store water safely for human consumption like drinking and cooking.

Rain Water Harvesting Tanks

Collect and store rainwater safely for reuse, reducing dependence on groundwater and promoting sustainability.

Fire Water Tanks

Used in industrial facilities and commercial buildings to provide a reliable water source for firefighting.

Agricultural Water Tanks

Store water for irrigation, livestock, and other farming needs, ensuring reliable supply during dry seasons.

Industrial Process Water Tanks

Designed for manufacturing, cooling systems, and industrial water management applications.

In today's world, water scarcity is a growing concern, especially in rapidly developing cities like Bangalore and Chennai. At TVK, we provide precast reinforced concrete water tanks that are durable, cost-effective, and ideal for installation even in congested urban environments. Our tanks are engineered for easy erection and maintenance, offering reliable and space-efficient solutions across India.

SPECIAL PURPOSE TANKS

Concrete precast tanks are used to store liquids other than water in industrial, commercial, and agricultural applications. These tanks are constructed using precast panels assembled on-site for efficient installation.

ETP Tanks

Essential for Effluent Treatment Plants to treat industrial wastewater before discharge or reuse.

Chemical Storage Tanks

Durable, corrosion-resistant containment for industrial chemicals.

Wastewater Treatment Tanks

Used in sewage and industrial systems for wastewater processing.

Septic Tanks

Separate solids from wastewater before further treatment or discharge.

Fertilizer Storage Tanks

Safely store fertilizers in agricultural and industrial settings.

Biogas Storage Tanks

Contain biogas safely under pressure while preventing leakage.

Oil Storage Tanks

Store industrial oils and lubricants securely with reinforced design.

Acid Storage Tanks

Designed for hazardous acid storage with high chemical resistance.

Each precast concrete tank is designed to meet the chemical and environmental requirements of the stored substances, ensuring durability, safety, and compliance with industry standards.
