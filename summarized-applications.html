SUMMARISED APPLICATIONS

Precast Tanks designed for industrial, commercial, and agricultural storage applications.

Type of Tanks

Type of Tank Capacity Applications
Small Domestic Tanks 500 Ltrs to 5000 Ltrs
  • Domestic and small industrial usages.
  • For water storage, septic systems, stormwater management, or wastewater treatment.
Monolithic Tanks 6 KL to 12.5 KL
  • Used in areas where large-volume of water storage is required.
  • Suitable for medium size residential and industries.
  • Can also be used as septic tanks, stormwater storage and management tanks, wastewater treatment tanks etc.
  • An array / battery of tanks also can be used by connecting them for higher volume requirements.
Custom Factory Assembled Tanks 15 KL to 45 KL
  • Suitable for projects where specific design considerations or environmental factors make off-site manufacturing a preferred option.
  • Tailored solution that meets the unique requirements of the application.
  • Commonly used in big Residential Complexes, Industries for storing the water and other purposes like storm water storage, wastewater treatment and industrial purposes.
  • An array / battery of tanks also can be used by connecting them for higher volume requirements.
Custom On-site Assembled Tanks More than 45 KL
  • Water storage, industrial processes, wastewater treatment, and more.
Special Purpose Tanks (To store other Liquids) As per the Client’s requirements
  • To store any liquid other than water.
  • Used for wastewater treatment, ETP, chemical storage, septic tanks, oil storage, biogas storage, acid storage, fertilizer storage, etc.

We manufacture Special Purpose Tanks of various capacities as per the clients’ requirements for storing liquids other than water used in various industrial, commercial, and agricultural applications.

If the size is small or medium, the entire unit is precast at factory and transported to the site for erection. If the size is larger (above 45 KL), these tanks are built using precast concrete panels cast in the factory and assembled on-site.

Each tank design adheres to environmental and safety standards to ensure strength, containment, and compliance with industry norms.
