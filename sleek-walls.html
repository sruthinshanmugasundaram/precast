PRECAST SLEEK WALL

Sleek Walls are thin concrete panels made using structural steel and high-strength concrete of M40 grade. Full composite action is achieved by the bonding of the concrete to the metal framing assembly. These are robust and highly flexible having wide variety of applications. It can be designed as load bearing walls, shear walls & as cladding walls. These walls have been designed as per Building Code requirements for Seismic Zones and for high wind loads.

SIZE OF SLEEK WALL

  • Can span up to 8m–9m in length.
  • Can be fixed vertically up to 6m as one single piece.
  • Can be welded or bolted for easy removal and relocation.
