KEY FEATURES

1. SOUND & HEAT INSULATION

HCS walls have excellent sound insulation properties. Due to the voids, heat transfer from exterior to interior is minimal.

2. ECO-FRIENDLY

HCS walls are resource-efficient. Longitudinal voids reduce raw material usage, making them more sustainable.

3. QUICK & SPEEDY EXECUTION

Precast components allow fast installation, reducing project timelines significantly.

4. CONSTRUCTION AT EASE

Simple design and modular panels make installation easy even in congested areas.

5. FIRE RESISTANCE

200 mm thick panels offer 120 minutes of fire resistance; this can be increased with design modifications.

6. RESISTANCE AGAINST WEATHER

The groove joint design prevents water ingress, improving weather resistance and lifespan.

7. THERMAL PROPERTY

The thermal resistance of the HCS wall enhances energy efficiency within the structure.
