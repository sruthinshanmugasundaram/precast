MATERIAL SPECIFICATION

Explore the premium materials used in TVK Precast structures — engineered for durability, strength, and precision in every project.

CONCRETE

M40 Grade Concrete delivers exceptional strength with a compressive value of 40 MPa, ensuring long-term durability for heavy-duty structures.

STEEL

Fe500 Grade Steel provides excellent yield strength and ductility, ideal for high-load applications in modern precast systems.

STRANDS

12.7mm LRPC Strands are used for post-tensioning walls and joining panels, ensuring structural stability and superior tensile performance.

JOINTING THE PANELS

Specialized Epoxy Adhesives provide seamless bonding of precast panels, creating watertight, high-strength joints.

WATER PROOFING

A blend of Nitto Bond, Cement, and Polymers ensures internal waterproofing, while an outer damp-proof coat enhances long-term protection.
