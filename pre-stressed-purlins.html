PRE-STRESSED PURLINS

Pre-stressed purlins from TVK are precast, high-strength roof/floor members designed for fast erection, long spans and reduced site work — ideal for industrial & commercial buildings.

Key Features

  • High span capability — covers larger bay widths with fewer supports.
  • Factory quality control — consistent strength & finish.
  • Faster erection — minimizes on-site formwork and curing time.
  • Lightweight for handling — easier transportation and placement.
  • Fire & weather resistant — durable service life.
  • Custom lengths & profiles — adapted to project needs.
