PRECAST CONCRETE FURNITURE

TVK’s range of Precast Concrete Furniture combines durability, functionality, and aesthetics — from kitchen counters and sinks to outdoor benches and tables, each product is crafted to last with minimal maintenance.

Precast Concrete Kitchen Counters

Precast concrete kitchen counters are custom sized and shaped to any kitchen configuration. Concrete doesn’t scratch and is impervious to heat, making it highly durable and long-lasting.

Precast Concrete Sinks

Precast concrete sinks blend durability with elegance, creating a seamless and modern look. They are part of concrete countertops and ideal for kitchens or wash areas.

  • Modified Wave Sink
  • Stretch Wave Sink

Precast Concrete Seaters

Providing you the best range of concrete seaters with the finest finish. All components are reinforced for long-term use and protection during handling and installation.

Suitable for:

  • Outdoor Gardens
  • Railway Stations
  • Smart City Seating
  • Parks

Precast Concrete Benches

Concrete benches are perfect for public and private spaces alike. Their heavy, stable structure prevents unwanted relocation while offering a stylish and sturdy seating solution.

Precast Concrete Bollards

These bollards provide impact protection and guide traffic efficiently. Their robust profile and weight make them ideal for high-traffic safety zones.

Suitable for:

  • Pedestrian Pathways
  • Roadsides & Parking
  • Building Entrances
  • Asset Protection Areas

Precast Concrete Tables

Concrete tables offer maximum durability with minimal maintenance. Their reinforced design ensures longevity, making them ideal for both indoor and outdoor use.

  • Deco Dining Table
  • Contempo Coffee Table
  • Blazer Coffee Table
