PRECAST CONCRETE COMPOUND WALL

FOR SAFE AND SECURE ENCLOSURE

TVK’s compound walls are engineered to suit different customer requirements. These walls are used as boundary walls, high-rise security walls with barbed wires or grills, or as simple low-cost walls for dividing boundaries between properties. Decorative walls are also available based on customer needs and budget.

Over the years, TVK has studied various customer requirements and developed reliable, durable, and cost-effective solutions that fit every application.

VARIOUS SIZES OF PRECAST CONCRETE COMPOUND WALL

The sizes of all precast compound walls vary based on the customer’s requirements, soil conditions, and land topography.

FOUNDATION DETAILS

The foundation type depends on soil condition and budget. Two main types of foundation are adopted:

  • Pile Foundation: Ideal for clayey soil and high water table. It uses a cast-in-place pile with a precast pile cap and socket.
  • Open Eccentric Foundation: Suitable for good soil conditions. It combines footing and pocket as one piece, eliminating cast-in-place work.

Topography of Land

Based on the land’s topography, the height of the compound wall varies. If the client maintains the same ground level, wall height remains constant. For leveled land, filling or cutting may be required, and retaining walls may be included for stability when fill exceeds 2m.

Other Features of the Walls

  • Decorative elements like wall coping and column coping can be added.
  • Safety features such as grills, Y-angles, and barbed wires can be installed.
  • Walls can be painted, polished, or engraved with labels or logos.

Advantages of TVK’s Solution

Very less site work and easy for any contractor to learn and erect the compound wall.

The walls are single panel, unlike other suppliers who offer many panels, ensuring a proper straight line.

The columns can be bolted to the foundation and relocated easily — ideal for landlords expanding their properties.
