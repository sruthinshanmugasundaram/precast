CUSTOM ON-SITE ASSEMBLED TANKS

Custom on-site assembled tanks refer to very large-capacity tanks that are assembled directly at the project site using precast components produced at our factory. Unlike factory-assembled tanks that arrive fully built, these tanks are constructed on-site, providing flexibility and scalability for large installations.

ADVANTAGE - CUSTOM ON-SITE ASSEMBLED TANKS

FLEXIBLE DESIGN

Allows full customization based on the project’s size, shape, and functional requirements.

PRECAST COMPONENTS

All side wall panels, slabs, and beams are precast at the factory for superior quality and accuracy.

TRANSPORTED TO SITE

Precast elements are safely transported to the site for efficient on-site assembly.

ON-SITE CONSTRUCTION

The final structure is assembled at site using bolting, welding, or other required methods.

ADAPTABLE TO TERRAIN

Ideal for locations with irregular ground levels, limited access, or special environmental conditions.

SEAMLESS INTEGRATION

Can be integrated with plumbing, electrical, or SCADA systems for smart monitoring.

STRONG FOUNDATION CONNECTION

Installed on a well-prepared foundation ensuring structural stability and long service life.

STRICT QUALITY CONTROL

Each stage—precasting, transport, and assembly—is monitored for quality and compliance.

WIDE APPLICATION

Used for water storage, industrial use, stormwater management, and wastewater treatment.

FASTER THAN CONVENTIONAL

Significantly quicker than traditional construction of large-capacity tanks.

PROJECT-SPECIFIC DECISION

Choice between on-site and factory assembly is based on project logistics and client needs.

CUSTOM ENGINEERING

Every tank is designed to match site conditions and required performance parameters.
